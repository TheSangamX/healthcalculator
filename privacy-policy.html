Your privacy is our priority

Last updated: January 2024

Quick Summary: We don't collect, store, or share your personal health data. All calculations are performed locally in your browser, and we respect your privacy completely.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

Health Calculator Pro does NOT collect any personal health information. When you use our BMI or Age calculators:

  • Your height, weight, and birth date are processed locally in your browser
  • No personal data is transmitted to our servers
  • No account creation or registration is required
  • We do not store your calculation results

Non-Personal Information

We may collect non-personal information to improve our service:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (mobile, desktop, tablet)
  • General location (country/region) for analytics
  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • Referral sources

2. How We Use Information

The limited non-personal information we collect is used to:

  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Understand which features are most popular
  • Optimize our content and calculators
  • Ensure our website works properly on different devices
  • Generate anonymous usage statistics

3. Local Storage

Our website may use local storage (browser storage) to:

  • Remember your calculator preferences
  • Store calculation history locally on your device
  • Improve your user experience

This information never leaves your device and can be cleared by clearing your browser data.

4. Cookies and Tracking

Essential Cookies

We use minimal essential cookies for:

  • Website functionality
  • Security purposes
  • Remembering your preferences

Analytics Cookies

We may use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This helps us:

  • Improve our website design
  • Create better content
  • Fix technical issues

You can opt-out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Third-Party Services

Google AdSense

We may display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to our site and other sites on the Internet.

Social Media Sharing

Our website includes social media sharing buttons. When you use these features, the respective social media platforms may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of information:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • No storage of sensitive personal health data
  • Secure hosting infrastructure

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Use our calculators without providing personal information
  • Clear your browser data and local storage at any time
  • Disable cookies in your browser settings
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns
  • Request information about data we may have collected

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Providing notice on our website if changes are significant

Remember: Your health data stays on your device. We're committed to protecting your privacy while providing you with valuable health calculation tools.
