Healthy BMI Range & BMI Categories

Understanding BMI Categories and Normal BMI Range

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight. Learn about healthy BMI range, normal BMI range, and BMI categories for optimal health. Our BMI calculator is specially designed for Indians and provides accurate BMI calculations.

BMI Categories & Healthy BMI Range:

Underweight (Below Normal BMI Range): Below 18.5
Normal BMI Range (Healthy BMI Range): 18.5 - 24.9
Overweight (Above Healthy BMI Range): 25.0 - 29.9
Obese (High BMI Health Risks): 30.0 and above

BMI for Indians - Special Considerations:

BMI for Indians may have different healthy ranges due to body composition differences. Consult healthcare professionals for personalized BMI interpretation and weight management advice.

Maintaining Healthy BMI

Regular exercise, balanced nutrition, adequate sleep, and stress management are key factors in maintaining a healthy BMI range.

BMI Limitations

BMI doesn't distinguish between muscle and fat mass. Athletes may have high BMI due to muscle mass, not excess fat.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is BMI accurate for everyone?

BMI is a useful screening tool but has limitations. It may not be accurate for athletes, elderly, or people with high muscle mass.

How often should I check my BMI?

For general health monitoring, checking BMI monthly or quarterly is sufficient unless advised otherwise by a healthcare provider.

Why calculate exact age?

Knowing your exact age helps with legal documents, insurance, medical records, and celebrating precise milestones.
